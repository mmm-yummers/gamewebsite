It's time to face your fears.

Are you brave enough to face the demons that lurk in the shadows? In this heart-pumping horror survival game, available now on Steam, you'll play as Lilith, an outcast with a fascination for the occult and a history of terrifying nightmares. When Lilith stumbles upon a mysterious artefact, she's transported to a hellish landscape where humanoid demons hunt humans for the thrill of fear. Solve puzzles, uncover dark secrets, and outwit the demons in intense chase scenes that will have you on the edge of your seat. Will she find a way home, or make a new home in this strange land?
